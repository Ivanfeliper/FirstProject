My 3 Favorite movies

#1 Castaway on the Moon

Directed by: Lee Hae-jun
Genre: Drama/Romance
Synopsis:
A young man decides to end everything by jumping into the Han River, but instead wakes up on a small island in the middle of the Seoul River.

#2 Seven Years in Tibet

Directed by: Jean-Jacques
Genre: Biographical War Drama Film
Synopsis:
It is based on Austrian mountaineer and Schutzstaffel (SS) officer Heinrich Harrer's 1952 memoir Seven Years in Tibet, about his experiences in Tibet between 1944 and 1951.

#3 The Experiment

Directed by: Paul T. Scheuring
Genre: Drama Thriller
Synopsis:
Volunteers arrive for a psychological study led by Dr. Archaleta (Stevens). Among the volunteers is Travis (Brody), a proud anti-war protestor, and Michael Barris (Whitaker), a 42-year-old man who still lives with his domineering mother.
